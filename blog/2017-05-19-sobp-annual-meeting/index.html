Talk at SOBP 2017

Posted on 2017, May 19 by Kirstie Whitaker

I’m very excited to present our work on structural brain development during adolescence at the Society of Biological Psychiatry 2017 annual meeting in San Diego.

My talk is part of a symposium on Network Architectures of the Healthy Brain as Constraints for the Spatiotemporal Patterning of Disease. We were brought together by chairs Armin Raznahan and Mallar Chakravarty and it should be a really fascinating afternoon session.

My slides are linked below, and you can read the paper I’m presenting at PNAS.

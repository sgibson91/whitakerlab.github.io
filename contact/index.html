Contact

Posted on 2021, May 18

There are lots of ways to get in contact with the Lab and we’re very happy to answer questions.

You can contact Kirstie at kwhitaker@turing.ac.uk or on twitter @kirstie_j.

The lab tweets @Whitaker_Lab. Give us a follow and let us know what you think.

You’re very welcome to contact us via GitHub too. Check out the issues at the lab’s project management repository. You may be able to help us out!

We have a gitter chat room and love to say hello there.

This website is hosted via GitHub pages. If you see any typos or other mistakes please let us know… or file a pull request with your edits.
